        Transparent Pricing Policy
        
          At DigitalOcean, we're on a mission to simplify the complexities of
          infrastructure and that includes pricing. We have a mantra internally
          called "keeping it DO-Simple" and we believe that all pricing models
          should be straightforward and predictable. That is why all Droplets
          are billed hourly, up until a monthly cap (672 hours or 28 days) so
          it's always February and there are no surprises at the end of the
          month when you receive your bill - it is that simple.
        
      
    
  
  
    
      
        
          
            What form of payments do you accept?
            
              We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and
              PayPal. For additional payment options, like purchase orders and
              ACH, please contact sales.
            
          
          
            Will taxes be included in my monthly invoice?
            
              Yes, if you live in Canada, India, or the EU. Please review the
              tax help sections on the Pricing & Billing page for more information.
            
          
        
      
      
        
          
            How does hourly pricing work?
            
              All Droplets are billed hourly, up until a monthly cap. We have
              estimated that each month has about 672 hours (28 days). If you
              use your server for less than 672 hours during the month, you will
              be billed for each hour that you used it. If you use your server
              for more than 672 hours that month, you will be billed at the
              monthly cost, which is no greater than 672 hours.
            
          
          
            Am I charged when I enter a credit card?
            
              No—when you enter a card, we only verify the card. Your card is
              charged at the end of the billing cycle.
